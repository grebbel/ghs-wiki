### Antwoorden

- **Q1 (firm boundary)**: Build / Compose / Collaborate / Buy Outcomes — 4 modellen, niet wederzijds uitsluitend. **22× groei** GenAI app-uitgaven 2023→2025 ($1.7B→$37B); SaaS-valuaties 30-60% onder 2021-piek; >1/3 vervangt SaaS met custom GenAI; 40% van code AI-gegenereerd. *"The boundary of the firm becomes a variable rather than a given."*
- **Q1+Q3 (synthese 10 frameworks)**: 10 frameworks; **"Models commoditize; what's owned compounds"** — drie onafhankelijke vantages convergeren (Chatterjee engineering / Nishar-Nohria firm-boundary / Kiron-Schrage organisatie). 7-15% top-tail cohort over 5 metingen; *"compounding cycle"* gelijk mechanisme op twee stack-lagen.
- **Q2 (durable skills)**: Collaboration / creativity / critical thinking als kanon (Globerson Vantage 2026, eerste schaalbare meting); expert-as-evaluator is geen transitierol; Polanyi tacit-dimension breach; AI-literacy als 5e durable skill of fluency.
- **Q2 (employment)**: Brynjolfsson canaries: -13% relatief voor 22-25 in AI-blootgestelde beroepen; **softwareontwikkelaars 22-25: -20% van piek**; automatisering-uses drijven dit, augmentatie-uses niet; +1.0-1.2 pp/yr aggregate productivity; **84% hoop vs 20% angst** (vs 70/31% in 2017) — incumbents veiliger, entrants smaller.

## Synthese — kernelementen verwerkt in essay

1. **Business models in 2050** projecteren vanuit Nishar-Nohria's Build/Compose/Collaborate/Buy Outcomes; 22× GenAI app-spend in 2 jaar als trajectory-anchor; harness als markt; outcome-economy spreading.
2. **Occupations** bouwen op Brynjolfsson canaries (-20% software developers 22-25), durable skills (collaboration/creativity/critical thinking), expert-as-evaluator (Kiron-Schrage), agentic engineering (Karpathy), Centaur/Cyborg (Dell'Acqua) — drie seniority-tiers gerangschikt naar wat AI niet vervangt.
3. **SME organization** projecteert Octopus + Rewired 6-capability + Augmented Learners + verification → evaluation → learning capture flywheel.
4. **Business architecture** mapt brain/hands/session (Anthropic Managed Agents) op firm-scale; harness 4 lagen als enterprise-laag; agent-native infrastructure (Karpathy).
5. **Most valuable assets/capitals** synthese: harness owned + wiki-as-organizational-codebase + evaluation-expertise + Augmented-Learner culture + relationship/customer capital + spec-design taste.
6. **Honest uncertainty marking**: Karpathy's neural-host-process flip is TBD; "everything is automatable" is delivered with laughter; the wiki has no 2050 sources; "It would be a mistake to declare the end of SaaS" (Nishar-Nohria); Kokane's "90% rebranded systems engineering" sceptische vantage gehandhaafd.
