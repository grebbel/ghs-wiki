# Logan Kilpatrick

Product lead for **Google AI Studio** and the **Gemini API** at [[Google DeepMind]]; previously led developer relations at OpenAI. Within this wiki he is the named source for the **"model eats the harness"** thesis and the **jagged-superintelligence** framing, via the [[2026-06-11-kilpatrick-sequoia-model-eats-the-harness|Sequoia "Why the Model Eats the Harness" interview]].

## What he is the wiki's source for

- **"The model eats the harness."** The most-cited claim from his [[2026-06-11-kilpatrick-sequoia-model-eats-the-harness|Sequoia interview]]: scaffolding runs ahead of the model, then the model digests it and the alpha moves elsewhere — "the harness, in the way we think of it today, perhaps won't be true in 12 months." A frontier-lab counterpoint to the wiki's [[agent-harness]] corpus (see that page's *Debates and supersession*).
- **Antigravity as Google's through line.** Describes [[Antigravity]] — born from the Windsurf team — as the single agent harness now powering search, the Gemini app, Cloud, and AI Studio.
- **Jagged / narrow superintelligence.** Coding already "feels like narrow superintelligence"; verifiable verticals (math, finance, science) will cross the bar before AGI.
- **Outcomes over eyeballs.** Argues Google's goal is maximizing user outcomes, not eyeball time.
