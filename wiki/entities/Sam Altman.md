# Sam Altman

**CEO of [[OpenAI]]** since 2019; **ex-President of [[Y Combinator]]** (2014–2019, predecessor to [[Garry Tan]]); co-founder of Loopt.

Surfaces in this wiki primarily for two framings:

1. **The *"AI washing"* counter-framing** — cited by [[Paul Everitt]] at [[2026-05-22-everitt-jetbrains-deeplearningai-ai-dev-26-sf-shift-to-agentic-engineering|AI Dev 26 SF]] as a counter-anchor to the *"more code fewer people"* mega-layoff narrative (Atlassian / Block / Stripe / Amazon, May 2026). Altman publicly accused some firms of *AI washing* — using AI as justification for headcount cuts that were planned independently. *"Sam Altman brings up another word into my vocabulary: AI washing. And so now I'm a cheerleader for Sam Altman."* The wiki's clearest single executive-altitude-public pushback on the AI-justifies-layoffs framing.

2. **The OpenAI-altitude AGI-bull framing** — Altman is referenced across multiple sources as one of the loudest *AGI within a few years* voices, alongside [[Dario Amodei]] and Geoff Hinton. Cited by [[Chad Jones]] at [[2026-05-21-jones-stanford-gsb-ai-and-our-economic-future|Stanford GSB]] as part of the *FOOM scenario* set Jones is arguing against.
