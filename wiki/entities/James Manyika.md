# James Manyika

Zimbabwean-American business executive and academic. Since April 2023 **Senior Vice President and President for Research, Labs, Technology & Society at [[Google]]–Alphabet**, reporting to Sundar Pichai. The role expanded an earlier 2022 appointment as SVP for Technology & Society and now oversees Google Research, Google Labs, and Google's responsible-AI work alongside Demis Hassabis (DeepMind) and Jeff Dean (Chief Scientist) — visible in the public record around the 2024–25 Gemini push.

Previously **Chairman and Director of [[McKinsey & Company|McKinsey Global Institute]]** (2009–2022), where he led decades of "future of work" research that anchors several wiki sources. PhD in robotics/AI from Oxford (Rhodes Scholar). Member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.

Public-policy roles relevant to the wiki's responsible-AI thread:

- **Vice-chair, U.S. National AI Advisory Committee (NAIAC)** — congressionally established body advising the White House (2022–April 2025).
- **Co-chair, UN High-Level Advisory Body on Artificial Intelligence** — appointed by the UN Secretary-General in October 2023, alongside Carme Artigas (former Spanish AI Minister).
- **Member, [[AI Index]] Steering Committee** — across multiple editions including 2025 and 2026.
