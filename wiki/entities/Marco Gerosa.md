# Marco Gerosa

Professor at **Northern Arizona University** and a software-engineering researcher who is the **lead academic author** of The Linux Foundation Research's **State of Tech Talent** survey series in this wiki. He authored both the global and the European 2026 reports with [[Adrienn Lawson]] of [[The Linux Foundation]].

## Wiki contributions

- [[2026-05-01-lf-state-of-tech-talent-global-2026|2026 State of Tech Talent (Global)]] — lead author (with [[Adrienn Lawson]] and Anna Hermansen). The "not a jobs crisis, but a skills crisis" thesis; net hiring +26%/+31%; the quantified upskilling-vs-hiring preference.
- [[2026-06-01-lf-state-of-tech-talent-europe-2026|2026 State of Tech Talent Europe]] — co-author (with [[Adrienn Lawson]]). The European cut; the −3% entry-level-contraction finding corroborating the [[2026-04-28-brynjolfsson-canaries-coal-mine|Brynjolfsson-canaries]] entry-level-decline thesis.
