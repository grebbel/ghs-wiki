# Matthew Kropp

**Managing Director & Senior Partner at [[Boston Consulting Group]]** and a BCG Henderson Institute author; lead voice in BCG's research on AI's organizational and labor-market impact. Promoted to an entity on 2026-06-15 on his second wiki appearance.

## Wiki contributions

- [[2026-05-06-kropp-bcg-hbr-dont-treat-ai-agents-like-employees|Kropp et al. 2026 (HBR)]] — lead author of the N=1,261 randomized experiment showing the *AI-as-employee framing* causes a 9pp accountability drop and more escalations; the wiki's sharpest anti-anthropomorphizing-AI source.
- [[2026-04-03-bcg-emerson-kropp-ai-will-reshape-more-jobs-than-it-replaces|Emerson, Kropp et al. 2026]] — co-author of *AI Will Reshape More Jobs Than It Replaces*: the six **AI Labor Disruption Segments**, the 50–55%-reshaped / 10–15%-eliminated split, and the substitution-vs-augmentation × demand-expandability model.

## Why he is an entity (promotion note)

Promoted on 2026-06-15: named author on **two** BCG wiki sources (the agents-as-employees HBR feature + the labor-disruption report), crossing the second-source threshold. Frequent co-authors in the wiki: [[Julie Bedard]], [[Lisa Krayer]], [[Megan Hsu]].
